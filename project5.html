Jeporady Parody

The aim of the project is to see how small pieces of code similar to the ones you write in the lab assignments may fit into a larger program. The game features a simple quiz competition in which up to three contestants are presented with questions from different categories of knowledge, and they must answer them to gain score ($). You may think of it as a modified version of Jeopardy, the famous TV game show.
