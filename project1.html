Aid Finder

An application dedicated to tackling challenges like food and housing insecurity by locating nearby resources such as food banks, homeless shelters, and community services. This app aims to connect individuals with essential support, fostering a stronger community and addressing the pressing needs of those facing food and housing challenges.

Used Java & XML in Android Studio to code all funtionalities and aesthetics respectively. Also conducted Heuristic User Evaluations to refine prototype and implemented a Google Maps API for navigation screen.
