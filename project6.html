Solidwork Design Project

Designed an innovative outdoor sprinkler system using SolidWorks CAD/CAM to mitigate wildfire damage in Kelowna, BC, demonstrating proficiency in digital design and translating it into a tangible prototype.

Our group applied problem-solving skills to create a functional solution from the Soliworks 3D prototype, contributing to the reduction of wildfire impact on buildings and surrounding areas.
